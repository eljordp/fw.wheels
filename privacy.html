Information We Collect

FW Wheels may collect contact details, shipping details, order information, vehicle fitment details, and messages you send by phone, text, Instagram, or checkout.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to process orders, check fitment, provide support, coordinate shipping, handle warranty or return requests, and respond to customer questions.

Payments

Payment details are handled by Stripe or another approved payment processor. FW Wheels does not store full card numbers on this site.

Sharing

Order information may be shared with payment processors, shipping carriers, manufacturers, distributors, or service providers needed to fulfill or support your order.
